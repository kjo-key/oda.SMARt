For Male Students

  • Shirt: Collared, button-up in neutral colors like white or blue.
  • Trousers: Black, grey, or navy blue, well-fitted.
  • Tie: May be required, matching school colors.
  • Jacket/Blazer: For formal occasions.
  • Shoes: Polished black or brown leather.

For Female Students

  • Shirt/Blouse: Collared, button-up in neutral colors.
  • Skirt/Trousers: Knee-length or trousers in black, grey, or navy.
  • Dress: Optional in some schools.
  • Blazer/Jacket: For formal occasions.
  • Shoes: Polished black or brown leather.
